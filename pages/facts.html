Random Facts

  • SpongeBob SquarePants first aired on May 1, 1999.
  • The show was created by marine science educator and animator Stephen Hillenburg.
  • SpongeBob lives in a pineapple under the sea in the fictional underwater city of Bikini Bottom.
  • He works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab, a fast-food restaurant owned by Mr. Krabs.
  • SpongeBob's best friend is Patrick Star, a pink starfish who lives under a rock.
  • Other main characters include Squidward Tentacles, Sandy Cheeks, and Plankton.
  • The show has won multiple awards, including Emmy Awards and Kids' Choice Awards.
  • SpongeBob is known for his optimistic and cheerful personality.
